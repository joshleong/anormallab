Stories Distribution

Hey Guys, just wanted to provide some thoughts on Dyna Stories distribution. The largest problem with the native version last year was getting it into everyone's hands especially if the initial version was read only.

Because Adam's prototype is on the web, it makes it very easy to integrate into something like GMail through a chrome extension, or Gmail addon.

In this prototype, we have access the the bar above emails, a custom page on the left hand pane, and the compose view.

Next Steps

In terms of immediate next steps I'll hook up the prototype to a jetway server to read all the stories that we may have, as you can see the interface really begs for more.

And I'll look into opening up stories modally within Gmail Instead of opening up a new tab.

Investigations

What might be interesting to look at down the road is full integration with GMail, as I suspect it's possible to read the emails, it might be possible to create stories within Gmail and also archive them as you watch stories.

The one caveat would be no mobile support, unless we look into Gmail add-ons which may not be as permissive as this system.
